Welcome To Safe Routes to School Online Database Management

This website allows you to manipulate the database that stores the walking data of all student participants. The users of this site have the ability to view the database according to different categories as well as the ability to add and delete students as necessary.

To get started, click on one of the tags near the top of this page. These will lead you to the essential pages of this website and allow you access to the database.

The Safe Routes to School RFID system was developed by students from Purdue University through the Engineering Projects in Community Service (EPICS) program. To read more about the group who developed this system and for contact information, click on the link below.
